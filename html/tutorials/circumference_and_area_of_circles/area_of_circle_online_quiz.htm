Q 1 - Find the area of following circle. Use π = 3.14

A - 132.67 square units

B - 314 square units

C - 200.96 square units

D - 706.5 square units

Answer : C

Explanation

Step 1:

Given diameter = 16 ; radius r = 16/2 = 8 units

Step 2:

Area of Circle = πr2 = (3.14)(8)(8) = 200.96 square units
