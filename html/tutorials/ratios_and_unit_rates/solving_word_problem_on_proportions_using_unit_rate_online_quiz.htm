Q 1 - A store sold 3 white chairs for $63.75. How much would 10 white chairs cost at the same unit price?

A - $212.00

B - $212.50

C - $213.50

D - $214.50

Answer : B

Explanation

Step 1:

Cost of 3 white chairs = $63.75

Unit price of white chairs = $$\frac{63.75}{3}$ = $$21.25$

Step 2:

Cost of 10 white chairs = $10 \times $$$21.25$ = $$212.50$

Q 2 - At a store potato chips cost $19.75 for 5 bags. What is the price of 8 bags at the same unit price?

A - $32.60

B - $31.40

C - $31.60

D - $31.80

Answer : C

Explanation

Step 1:

Cost of 5 bags of potato chips = $19.75

Unit price of a potato chips bag = $$\frac{19.75}{5}$ = $$3.95$

Step 2:

Cost of 8 bags of potato chips = $8 \times$ $$3.95$ = $$31.60$
