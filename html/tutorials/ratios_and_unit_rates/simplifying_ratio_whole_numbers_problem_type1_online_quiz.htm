Q 1 - Reduce the ratio 10:26 to its lowest terms.

A - 5:13

B - 5:19

C - 5:26

D - 10:13

Answer : A

Explanation

Step 1:

Ratio 10:26 in fraction form is $\frac{10}{26}$

Step 2:

To simplify the fraction, we divide numerator and denominator with the HCF of 10 and 26, which is 2 $\frac{10}{26} = \frac{10 \div 2}{26 \div 2} = \frac{5}{13}$.

$\frac{5}{13}$ in ratio form is 5:13

Step 3:

So, 10:26 in its lowest terms is 5:13
