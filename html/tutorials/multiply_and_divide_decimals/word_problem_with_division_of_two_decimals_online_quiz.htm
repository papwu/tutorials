Q 1 - There are 2.54 centimeters in one inch. How many inches are there in 70.64 centimeters?

A - 27.6 inches

B - 27.8 inches

C - 27.9 inches

D - 28.8 inches

Answer : B

Explanation

Step 1:

Number of centimeters in one inch = 2.54

Step 2:

Number of inches in 70.64 cm = 70.64/2.54 = 27.8

Q 2 - Gilbert makes $8.95 an hour. He grossed $353.53 last week. How many hours did he work?

A - 37.5 hours

B - 39.6 hours

C - 38.5 hours

D - 39.5 hours

Answer : D

Explanation

Step 1:

Gross earnings = $353.53

Earnings per hour = $8.95

Step 2:

Number of hours worked = 353.53/8.95 = 39.5

Q 3 - What is the average number of miles per day Damien drove on a 1309.75-mile trip done in 6.5 days?

A - 200.5 miles

B - 211.5 miles

C - 201.5 miles

D - 202.5 miles

Answer : C

Explanation

Step 1:

Distance of the trip 1309.75miles

Number of days = 6.5

Step 2:

Distance travelled per day = 1309.75 / 6.5 = 201.5 miles

Q 4 - A bottle of medicine contains 32.76 ounces. I take 1.56 ounces each dose. How many doses are in the bottle?

A - 21.0 doses

B - 21.2 doses

C - 22.0 doses

D - 22.0 doses

Answer : A

Explanation

Step 1:

Total ounces of medicine = 32.76

ounces in one dose = 1.56

Step 2:

Number of doses of medicine = 32.76/1.56 = 21

Q 5 - School lunches cost $236.25 for 17.5 weeks. About how much would the lunches cost per week?

A - $13.60

B - $13.70

C - $14.50

D - $13.50

Answer : D

Explanation

Step 1:

Total cost of lunches = $236.25

Number of weeks = 17.5

Step 2:

Cost per lunch 236.25/17.5 = $13.50

Q 6 - Richards has 9.75 kg coffee. He wants 0.25 kg bags. How many bags does he need?

A - 38 bags

B - 39 bags

C - 29 bags

D - 37 bags

Answer : B

Explanation

Step 1:

Total weight of the coffee = 9.75 Kg

Weight of one bag = 0.25 Kg

Step 2:

Number of bags = 9.75/0.25 = 39

Q 7 - A school track team member ran for a total of 155.25 miles in practice over 57.5 days. About how many miles did he average per day?

A - 2.7 miles

B - 2.8 miles

C - 2.9 miles

D - 2.6 miles

Answer : A

Explanation

Step 1:

Total distance run over 57.5 days = 155.25 miles

Step 2:

Distance run in one day = 155.25/57.5 = 2.7 miles

Q 8 - Noah's car travels 277.35 miles the highway on one full tank. If his fuel tank holds 10.75 gallons, then how far can he travel on one gallon of gas?

A - 24.8 miles

B - 26.8 miles

C - 25.8 miles

D - 25.9 miles

Answer : C

Explanation

Step 1:

Distance travelled in 10.75 gallons = 277.35 miles

Step 2:

Distance travelled in one gallon = 277.35/10.75 = 25.8 miles
