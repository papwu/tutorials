Q 1 - Express the following sentence into a one-step equation.

(Hint: Use x as the unknown number)

Six times a number minus 3 equals 75

A - 8x – 3 = 75

B - 6x – 3 = 75

C - 6x – 3 = 72

D - 6x – 4 = 75

Answer : B

Explanation

Step 1:

Let the number be x.

Step 2:

Six times the number = 6x

Six times a number minus 3 = 6x – 3

Step 3:

Therefore, 'Six times a number minus 3 equals 75' translated into one-step equation is

6x – 3 = 75
