Q 1 - Amit sold an umbrella for Rs. 680, losing 15%. The expense cost of the umbrella is:

A - Rs. 782

B - Rs. 800

C - Rs. 578

D - none of these

Explanation

Let the C.P. be Rs x =Then, S.P. = 85% of Rs x.
∴ 85/100 * x =680 ⇒ x = (680*20/17) =800.
∴ C.P. =Rs 800.
