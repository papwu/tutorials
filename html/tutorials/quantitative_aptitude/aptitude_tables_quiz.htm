Q 1 - If the expenditure of company U in the year 2003 was Rs.816000, what was the profit made by it in that year ?

A - Rs. 21830

B - Rs. 359040

C - Rs. 416840

D - none of these

Answer : B

Explanation

Percent profit of company U in the year 2003 =44%
Let the income be Rs. x then, (x-816000)/816000*100 =44
⇒ x- 816000 = 8160*44 ⇒ x =(359040+ 816000) = 1175040
∴ Profit = Rs. (1175040- 816000) = Rs.  359040
