C - Rs. 11000

A : B : C = 21000 : 17000: 12000 =  21: 17: 12
Share of A = Rs. (35000* 21/50) = Rs. 14700

If annual profit Rs. 27000 is announced then what will be the share of profit in the favour of C?

A - Rs. 8625

B - Rs. 9000

C - Rs. 10800

Explanation

If x , 2x, 3x amount invested by A, B and C respectively.

A - Rs. 4000

D - Rs. 4800

Explanation

Here x denote capital  and y denote   total time. Then,
Rs. x/6 for y/6 months for the investment of A  and Rs. x/3 for y/3 months  for the investment of B.
Investment of C = [ x-(x/6+ x/3)] = Rs. x/2 for y months.
Then , A: B: C = ( x/6 * y/6) : (x/3* y/3) : (x/2: y) = xy/36 : xy/9 : xy/2 =  1:4: 18
Share of  B in profit =  Rs. (23000* 4/ 23) = Rs. 4000.

And Sita gets = Re 237.5

Q 9 - Ram starts a business with Re 2 lakhs. Four months later Shyam joins him with rs 4 lakhs. Hari joins them for last three months with a capital of Re 6 lakhs. The profit at the year end is Re 7400. Find the share of each in the profit.

A - 2000, 3000, 2400

B - 2200, 3400, 1800

C - 2400, 3200, 1800

D - 3000, 3400, 2000

Answer : C

Explanation

Ram's contribution is for 12 months.
Shyam's contribution is for 8 months.
Hari's contribution is for 3 months.

Ram's contribution of Re 2 lakhs for 12 months is equivalent to Re (2*12) = Re 24 lakhs in 1 month
Shyam's contribution is equivalent to Re (4*8) = Re 32 lakhs in 1 month
Hari's contribution is equivalent to Re (6*3) = Re 18 lakhs in 1 month

Share in the profit should be in the ratio = 24 : 32 : 18
= 12 : 16 : 9
Sum of ratios = 12 +16 + 9 = 37
Ram's share = 12/37 * 7400 = Re 2400
Shyam's share = 16/37 * 7400 = Re 200
Hari's share = 9/37 * 7400 = Re 1800
